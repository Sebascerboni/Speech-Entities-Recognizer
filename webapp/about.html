I am Kiwilius! (っ ◕‿◕ )

I am a resource-rich 10-language model (Arabic, German, English, Spanish, French, Italian, Latvian, Dutch, Portuguese, and Chinese) based on a fitted BERT base model. It has been trained to recognize three types of entities:

  • Location (LOC)
  • Organizations (ORG)
  • Persons (PER)

Specifically, this model is a bert-base-multilingual-cased model that was fitted on a resource-heavy 10-language aggregation.
